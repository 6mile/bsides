About Gold Coast BSides

Gold Coast BSides is a one day event held at COHORT in Southport. BSides is a community run and community-focused cybersecurity conference. It will have one track of between 8 and 10 talks.

Audience

This is an inclusive community-focused event.

As this is our first year we wanted to start small so there will be a maximum of 100 tickets sold for this event.

Chatham House Rule

When a meeting is held under the Chatham House Rule, participants are free to use the information received, but the identity or affiliation of speakers and participants may not be revealed.

A Community Event

Gold Coast BSides is a community effort. It has been hosted by various companies who strive to keep it going in order to provide a forum for discussion among cybersecurity practitioners on the Gold Coast.
